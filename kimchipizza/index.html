Want to ferment your own food but not sure where to begin? Now is your chance to learn three different fermentation techniques in one workshop.

Learn how to make kimchi, sourdough starter, mozzarella, and Neopolitan-style pizza in this 3-in-1 fermentation workshop. We will be cooking pizza too!

You will leave with the basic skills to start making your own fermented vegetables, sourdough starter, and cheese at home. Plus, you will take home your own jar of fresh kimchi.

Whether you want to ultimately make all three ferments at home or just one, come for an entertaining and informative afternoon. History, science, hands-on step-by-step how-to, and more. We will walk through the different ferments as we prepare kimchi pizza, culminating in a group pizza "competition" and kim-chee-zza party!
